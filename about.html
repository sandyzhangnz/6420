About Us

Auckland City Books was established in 1971 and our book shops are in every little town in New Zealand. We stock books on a wide variety of subjects including Art, Drama, Humour, Music, Photography, Biography, Sports, Business, Careers, Investing, Cooking and Food, Education, Fiction and Poetry, Travel, Health and Self-Help, Medicine, Nutrition, Psychology, Recovery and Addiction, Relationships, Gardening, Interior Design, Sustainable Living, Humanities.
